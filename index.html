Private Interior Design Research Tool

App Description

What the app does

Read authorized boards

The app connects only after Pinterest OAuth authorization and reads boards and pins from the authorized Pinterest account.

Analyze design references

It supports private analysis of visual inspiration patterns such as interior style, material cues, color families, spatial mood, and repeated motifs.

Support creative workflow

The output is used by the account owner for interior design and architecture research, concept development, and personal knowledge organization.

Access Boundary

Read-only by design

The app requests read-only Pinterest API scopes for the authorized account. It does not create, edit, delete, move, publish, or save Pinterest content.

  • Requestedboards:read
  • Requestedpins:read
  • Requesteduser_accounts:read
  • Not requestedboards:write
  • Not requestedpins:write

This is a private research utility for the account owner. It is not a social media platform, content redistribution service, Pinterest clone, automation publishing tool, or advertising network.
