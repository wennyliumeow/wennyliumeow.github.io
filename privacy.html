Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Purpose

Wenny Liu Design Research provides a private read-only tool for analyzing Pinterest boards and pins authorized by the Pinterest account owner. The tool supports interior design and architecture research by identifying patterns in style, materials, color palettes, spatial atmosphere, and related design references.

Data accessed

After Pinterest OAuth authorization, the tool may access basic account information, board metadata, pin metadata, and pin image references available through the Pinterest API for the authorized account.

How data is used

Pinterest data is used only to provide read-only analysis requested by the authorized account owner. The data is not sold, shared with advertising networks, combined with other users' accounts, or used to create a public social media service.

Read-only access

The tool does not create, edit, delete, move, publish, or save Pinterest content. It does not request Pinterest write permissions such as boards:write or pins:write.

Storage

Analysis may be stored locally for the account owner's private research workflow. Access credentials are kept private and are not shared with third parties.
